Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers before you reach out:

Do you offer custom embroidery near Onalaska, TX?

Yes! Lakeside Ink & Threadz is located in Onalaska, Texas and offers custom embroidery on hats, shirts, jackets, patches, and other fabric items. We serve customers throughout East Texas including Livingston, Coldspring, Huntsville, Cleveland, Splendora, Lufkin, Point Blank, and Shepherd.

What is DTF printing and do you offer it locally?

DTF stands for Direct-to-Film. It's a printing method that transfers full-color designs directly onto fabric — perfect for detailed, photographic, or multi-color artwork on shirts. Yes, L.I.T. offers DTF printing locally in Onalaska, TX.

Can you help with business branding and logo design?

Absolutely. We offer full business branding services including original logo design, branded uniforms, custom apparel packs, and promotional items — perfect for local businesses wanting a professional, cohesive look.
