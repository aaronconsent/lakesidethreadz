Team Uniforms in Onalaska, TX | Lakeside Ink & Threadz

Custom embroidered uniforms for sports teams, school athletics, and recreational leagues. Built to perform and made to last.

Game day looks better when the whole team matches. At Lakeside Ink & Threadz, we create custom embroidered team uniforms for youth sports, adult leagues, school athletics, and company teams across the Lake Livingston area. Embroidery adds a premium touch that screen printing can't match — crisp logos, individual names, and player numbers that won't peel or fade through a season of games and washes. We've outfitted teams from Onalaska ISD to recreational softball leagues, and we know deadlines matter when the season's about to start.

Team uniform pricing depends on the garment type and customization level. Basic embroidered team shirts run $15-25 each, while full jerseys with names and numbers range $25-40. Volume discounts apply at 12+ pieces. Contact us with your roster size for exact pricing.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a team uniform order take?

Plan for 10-14 business days from approval for full team orders. If your season starts soon, contact us immediately — we can often accommodate rush orders for an additional fee.

Can you add individual player names to each uniform?

Absolutely. We embroider individual names and numbers on each piece. Just provide us your roster with sizes, names, and numbers.

Do you offer matching coach and parent gear?

Yes — we can create coach polos, parent t-shirts, and fan gear that coordinates with the team uniforms. Great for building team spirit.

What sports do you make uniforms for?

All of them. Baseball, softball, basketball, soccer, volleyball, golf, fishing teams, bowling — if your team needs matching gear, we can make it.

Can we reorder for new players mid-season?

Yes. We keep your digitized designs on file, so adding uniforms for new players is quick and easy. Typically faster than the original order.

Do you work with school teams?

We work with school athletic programs, booster clubs, and UIL teams across the region. We're familiar with Onalaska ISD, Livingston ISD, and other local schools.
